About Riverside AQuettes
We were founded in 1958 and became a non-profit corporation in 1966.   We are one of the oldest synchronized swimming clubs in the United States.  Our head coach is an internationally renowned swim coach.   Many of our girls have gone on to the Olympics , most recently 4 who were on the 2012 Olympic team.  Water Aerobics & Aqua Therapy were added in 2010 to round out our program.  We now offer every level of swim instruction from learning to swim to being a graceful Aquette, to assisting seniors in staying healthy as they age.
Lifetime of coaching, coordinating, and consulting in both Canada and USA. Olympic Coach, Coach of the Year  and a community leader in promoting girls health and fitness.  More info: click here
Linda has been teaching water aerobics for 21 years, aerobics for 32 years and loves helping people reach their personal best. AEA and AFAA certified as an instructor and personal trainer
