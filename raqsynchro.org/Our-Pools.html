Our Pools
We are very honored to have the use of the City of Riverside swimming pools.
There are three heated pools that we regularly use during the year for all of our activities.  Our main pool is Shamel Pool.  On the weekends we practice at both Shamel Pool and Sippy Woodhead Pool and for special events we use the brand new, state of the art Riverside Aquatic Complex at the Riverside City College Campus.
